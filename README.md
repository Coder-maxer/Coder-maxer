## <img src="https://api.iconify.design/tabler:user-code.svg?color=%2396825f&amp;height=26" height="26" alt="" />&nbsp; About me

I'm a **data scientist** with a **computer science** degree who kept gravitating toward the "actually ship it" side of the work. I like turning messy data into clear decisions, building **custom software and databases** from a blank file, and shipping products that are fast and easy to find. CHAD is the two-person studio I founded and run as CEO, with my brother as co-founder. It's where I get to do all three, and I'm still a little amazed I get to call it a job.
